Biography

He has reached impressive achievements for multimedia content analysis, retreival, generation and communication with continuous contributions to the related communities by developing algorithms, building new datasets, organizing contests, and build benchmarks. He has published 100+ papers in the first-tier venues. He holds 40+ Chinese patents and 2 U.S. patents. Several patents have been transferred to companies. Many novel techniques have been widely utilized for multimodal big data analysis for social network, smart city, and maritime space.
